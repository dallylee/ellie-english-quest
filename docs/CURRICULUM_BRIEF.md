# Curriculum Brief

## Learner level

CEFR A1 moving toward early A1+.

## Language profile

Eli can understand simple everyday English but may freeze when asked to produce longer answers. Activities should be controlled and scaffolded.

## Core A1 topics

1. My room and house
2. Food and breakfast
3. School and subjects
4. Daily routine and time
5. Transport and going to school
6. Family and friends
7. Free time and hobbies
8. Past time / last weekend

## Key weak areas to practise

- Everyday vocabulary gaps:
  - subject
  - blanket
  - window
  - cereal
  - household items
  - school subjects
- Sentence frames:
  - There is / there are
  - I like / I don’t like
  - For breakfast I have
  - My favourite subject is
  - I go to school by
  - I have got
  - Yesterday I went
- Time:
  - o’clock
  - half past
  - quarter past
  - quarter to

## Personalised vocabulary

- juhica = grandma’s soup, pronounced YOO-hee-tsa
- subject = predmet
- blanket = dekica
- Bjelovar, London, Zagreb
- singing, dancing, hip hop
- Maths, Music, Informatics

## Feedback style

Always:
1. praise
2. correct gently
3. give a simple model
4. reward effort

Examples:
- “Nice!”
- “Good try.”
- “A better sentence is: I like music.”
- “You got a star.”
